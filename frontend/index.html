Welcome to Deadman Switch!

This app is conceived for a person, the whistleblower, who wants to link his "life end" to the revelation of a secret password (possibly encrypting sensitive data). The secret password is stored securely on several Nano Ledger devices, which are then distributed to a set of trusted keepers. The keepers can use the devices to check whether the whistleblower is still "alive" anytime. In case of "death", the secret password will be revealed to them.

Select your role:

▷ You are the whistleblower

▷ You are a trusted keeper

Register your secret

In order to prove that you're alive, you're required to keep recording transactions on the bitcoin blockchain with a time lapse less than a time period you define. Therefore, on top of your secret password, some additional parameters must be provided to configure your deadman switch.

The bitcoin public key that will identify the transactions that "keep you alive".

The maximum amount of time (in seconds) that can pass between two consecutive transactions before the switch is activated, therefore enabling disclosure of your secret password to trusted keepers.

The hash of the block that contains your first transaction (to minimize the possibility of that block becoming orphan, wait for five confirmations).

Your secret password. It can be any combination of alphanumeric characters.

Click the check button to see if the whistleblower is still alive.
